Jump to Ping Pong History

Ping pong, also known as table tennis, is a fast-paced and exciting sport enjoyed worldwide.

Ping Pong History and Facts

  1. Origins
    • Invented in England, late 1800s
    • Originally played as a parlour game
    • Used makeshift equipment like books and cigar box lids
  2. Growth of the Sport
    • Standardized rules created in 1926
    • International Table Tennis Federation (ITTF) founded same year
    • Included in the Olympics since 1988
  3. Fun Facts
    • Fastest recorded ping pong ball speed is 116 km/h
    • Table tennis balls are only 2.7 cm in diameter
    • China has dominated international competitions for decades
